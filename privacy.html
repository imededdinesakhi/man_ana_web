Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location. However, our application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device.

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These third-party ad networks may collect and use non-personal data, such as your device's Advertising ID, to personalize ads and analyze ad performance.

For more information, please review Google’s privacy policy: Google Privacy & Terms.

3. Data Storage

The game saves your progress, level count, and coins locally on your device or via standard secure web storage. We do not store this data on external servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed to be safe and enjoyable for family audiences. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at your developer account support.
